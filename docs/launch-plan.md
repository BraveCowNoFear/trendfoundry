# TrendFoundry Launch Plan

## Goal

Get the first paid customer for a weekly AI/developer creator-intelligence pack without waiting for custom platform accounts.

## Target Buyers

- Bilibili tech creators who publish AI tools, coding agents, productivity, or developer workflow content.
- YouTube creators covering AI tools, open-source projects, coding agents, and technical explainers.
- Small creator teams that need a weekly topic pipeline.

## Initial Offer

TrendFoundry Weekly AI Creator Brief:

- 12 ranked opportunities.
- Source links and why each source matters.
- Bilibili title options.
- YouTube title options.
- Hook and recording outline.
- Thumbnail prompt.
- One ready-to-record opening script.

## Cold Start Channels

- GitHub: publish a public sample repo with `daily-brief.md` and the static demo page.
- Bilibili: post a free sample video made from `docs/ready-to-record-script.md`.
- YouTube: publish the same sample as a short practical test.
- Reddit/Hacker News-adjacent communities: share as a free weekly sample, not spam.
- Direct outreach: send a concise sample to 20 small AI/dev creators with a custom first idea.

## No-Account-Needed Assets Already Ready

- Product README in English and Chinese.
- Daily brief sample.
- Ready-to-record script.
- Static demo page.
- Sales page copy.
- Local daily automation.

## Account-Gated Assets To Create Later

- Payment link: Gumroad, Ko-fi, Lemon Squeezy, Stripe Payment Link, or Buy Me a Coffee.
- Email delivery: Buttondown, Beehiiv, Substack, or simple Gmail automation.
- Public repo and landing page hosting: GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages.

## Outreach Message

Subject: A source-backed AI video idea for your next upload

Hi, I built a small weekly brief that turns GitHub/HN/arXiv signals into Bilibili/YouTube-ready video ideas.

Here is one free example for your channel:

Topic: {topic}
Why now: {source-backed reason}
Suggested title: {title}
Opening hook: {hook}

If useful, I can send 12 of these every week with source links, outlines, and thumbnail prompts.

## Success Metrics

- 1 paid sample sale.
- 5 email replies from creators.
- 20 landing page visits from public posting.
- 1 creator records a video based on a TrendFoundry idea.
